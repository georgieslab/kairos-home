Built in Austria

From Coffee Shop to AI Innovation

The story of how a philosophy student working at a bakery built the future of journaling with Claude AI.

🇦🇹 Made in Austria

"Everyone should be able to write their own book"

At 30, I took a leave from university (High School Teachers' Education—Informatics & English) to pursue a dream. Working part-time at a local coffee shop in a small Austrian village to fund development, I read Marcus Aurelius' Meditations and watched my mother journal every day.

I thought: what if everyone could write their own Meditations? What if AI could help us understand our patterns like Marcus understood his?

"I had zero traditional coding skills. Just a clear vision and Claude AI as my development partner. Together, we built the entire app—45 journey paths, voice recording, NFC integration, everything. This is proof that you don't need to code to build technology. You need to understand people."

From chemistry student in Georgia → car accident → moving to Austria at 26 → 2 years learning German → starting a new degree → taking the leap to build Καιρός. Every struggle taught me something: resilience, patience, vision over perfection.
